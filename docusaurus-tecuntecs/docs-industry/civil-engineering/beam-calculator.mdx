# Beam Calculator

**Calculate the area of steel for beam section**

## Data

$$
f'_c \hspace{0.5cm} \text{strength of concrete}
$$

$$
f_y \hspace{0.5cm} \text{strength of steel}
$$


$$
b \hspace{0.5cm} \text{width of the beam base}
$$

$$
h \hspace{0.5cm} \text{beam height}
$$

$$
c_{e} \hspace{0.5cm} \text{effective cover}
$$

$$
M \hspace{0.5cm} \text{bending moment}
$$

## Calculations

### Effective height

$$
h - c_{e} = d \hspace{0.5cm} \text{effective height (or depth)}
$$


### Area of steel $[\text{cm}^2]$

$$
A_s = 0.85 b d \frac{F_c}{F_y} - \sqrt{(0.85 b d \frac{F_c}{F_y})^2 - 1.888889 M (100) F_c \frac{b}{F_y^2}}
$$

$$
A_{s,\text{min}}=\frac{14.1}{F_y b h}
$$

$$
A_{s,\text{max}}=\frac{0.5}{P_{bal} b d} \hspace{0.5cm} P_{bal} = 0.85 \frac{F_c}{F_y} \left( \frac{6000}{6000+F_y}\right) 0.75
$$
