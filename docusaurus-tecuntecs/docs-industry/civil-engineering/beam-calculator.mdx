## Beam Design in 10 Steps

When it comes to the design of beams, there are a few key steps:

#### 1. **Assume a beam section (also called "pre-dimensioning")**
$$
b \hspace{0.5cm} \text{width of the beam base}
$$

$$
h \hspace{0.5cm} \text{beam height}
$$

The effective depth of the beam will be determined by

$$
h - c_{e} = d \hspace{0.5cm} \text{effective height (or depth)}
$$


where $c_e$ is the effective cover of the beam, which is equal to
the nominal cover $c_n$ plus half of the diameter ($\text{dia}$)
of the main reinforcement of the beam.

$$
c_n + \frac{1}{2}\text{dia} = c_{e} \hspace{0.5cm} \text{effective cover}
$$


$$
c_{n} \hspace{0.5cm} \text{nominal cover}
$$
