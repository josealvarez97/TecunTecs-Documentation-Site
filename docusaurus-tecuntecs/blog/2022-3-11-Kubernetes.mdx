Kubernetes is one of the best solutions to leverage GPUs in platforms such as Google Cloud, but although Kubernetes has become the standard for orchestrating software in the cloud, the [learning curve](https://medium.com/google-cloud/kubernetes-101-pods-nodes-containers-and-clusters-c1509e409e16) is stil very steep, specially for those without software engineering backgrounds.

To illustrate, there follows a few of the basic concepts that make up a Kubernetes system:

- Nodes
- Clusters
- Pods
- Deployments
- Ingress
